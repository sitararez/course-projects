Sample Walkthrough: Mystic Manor

You awaken on a silent train, with no memory of how you arrived. The sky is heavy with dark clouds as the train grinds to a halt. The doors slide open, and an unseen force propels you out. As you gather yourself and look back, the train has already vanished into the distance. Before you lies a desolate wilderness, vast and unsettling. In the distance, a forsaken manor stands, shrouded in mystery. Beyond it, the horizon stretches empty and unknown. Do you dare enter the manor, or lose yourself in the wilderness? The choice is yours, the first of many in this unfolding tale.
